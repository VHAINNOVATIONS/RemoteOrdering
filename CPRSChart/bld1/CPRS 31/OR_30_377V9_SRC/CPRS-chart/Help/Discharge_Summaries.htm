Discharge Summaries

Authorized users can write, review, edit, save, and sign discharge summaries for individual patients through CPRS or through the Text Integration Utilities (TIU) package. With CPRS, you open one patient record at a time and can sign the discharge summary. If you want to review, write, edit, or sign for multiple patients at one time, you must use TIU.

To make entry of discharge summary easier, you can create and use document templates that contain text and TIU objects. Once you have created the templates, documenting visits, admissions, and consults should be much faster.
